# GTM Plan: CSV Export

## Positioning
**For:** Engineering leads who report to non-technical stakeholders
**The problem:** Sharing project status means manual copy-paste into spreadsheets
**Our solution:** One-click CSV export from any project - ready for Google Sheets or Excel in seconds
**Why us:** It's already in Trackr, where your tasks live - no extra tools, no manual work

## Audience
**Primary:** Existing active teams (200 teams in beta) - especially those with non-technical stakeholders
**Secondary:** Enterprise trial teams (3 active) who cited "no export" as a blocker

## Launch approach
Soft launch - no press, no big announcement. Ship it, tell existing users, watch adoption.

## Channels and tactics

### 1. In-app changelog (launch day)
- One-line entry in the in-app changelog: "CSV export - export any project's tasks with one click."
- Link to a 60-second Loom demo

### 2. Email to active teams (launch day + 1)
- Subject: "You can now export your tasks to CSV"
- Body: 3 sentences + GIF showing the button → download → Google Sheets
- Segment: teams with >5 tasks in the last 30 days (most likely to use it)

### 3. Discord announcement (launch day)
- Post in #announcements: same message as email, shorter
- Pin for 1 week

### 4. Twitter/X (launch day)
- One tweet: "CSV export is live in Trackr. Export any project's tasks to a spreadsheet in one click. [link]"
- No thread, no hype - just the fact

### 5. Direct outreach to enterprise trials (launch day)
- Personal email from Alex to the 3 enterprise trial contacts who mentioned export
- "The thing you asked for is live - here's how to use it"

## Timing
- Ship: end of March (target)
- All channels fire same day as ship
- Follow-up email at day 7 if adoption is below 10%

## Success metrics
- 20% of active teams use export within 30 days
- Support tickets about "how do I share this?" drop by 50% within 30 days
- At least 1 enterprise trial converts and cites export

## Risks
- Low adoption if teams don't know the button exists - mitigated by in-app changelog + email
- Enterprise trials may have already churned - check status before outreach
